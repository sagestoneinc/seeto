About Seeto Realty

Building relationships, creating success since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Seeto Realty was born from a vision to revolutionize real estate services in Texas. What started as a small boutique brokerage in Plano has grown into a trusted name across the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex and Houston.

For over 13 years, we've been committed to providing exceptional service, expert guidance, and personalized attention to every client. Our success is built on integrity, market knowledge, and a genuine passion for helping people achieve their real estate dreams.

Today, with offices in both Plano and Houston, we continue to expand our reach while maintaining the personal touch that has always defined us.
